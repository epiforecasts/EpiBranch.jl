# EpiBranch.jl — Design

The architectural design lives in [`docs/src/design.md`](docs/src/design.md) so it is published as part of the documentation. Start there — it covers the offspring/timing/competing-risks separation, the extension model, the abstract-type naming convention, the reserved `Individual.state` keys, the four intervention hooks, time-based scheduling, analytical composition, and the simulation/mutation/automatic-differentiation discussion.

The complementary [`docs/src/principles.md`](docs/src/principles.md) lists the five high-level principles the design is judged against.
